Job Title: Product Manager

We are seeking a Product Manager with 3+ years of experience to lead product discovery and delivery for our web and mobile applications.

Requirements:
- 3+ years of product management or related experience
- Proficient in writing product requirements and roadmapping
- Experience with Agile development methodology and tools like Jira
- Basic data analytics and querying skills (SQL preferred)
- Strong user research and customer discovery skills
- Excellent communication, presentation, and collaboration skills
- Bachelor's or Master's degree in Business, CS, or related field

Nice to have:
- Experience in B2B SaaS
- Basic understanding of web development technologies

Responsibilities:
- Define product vision, strategy, and detailed feature requirements
- Prioritize the product backlog and collaborate with engineering and design teams
- Conduct user research, market analysis, and analyze key metrics to guide decisions
