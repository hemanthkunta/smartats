Job Title: Mobile iOS/Android Developer

We are seeking a Mobile Developer with 3+ years of experience building responsive, native or cross-platform mobile applications for iOS and Android.

Requirements:
- 3+ years of experience in mobile application development
- Proficiency in native languages (Swift for iOS / Kotlin/Java for Android) or cross-platform frameworks (React Native)
- Solid understanding of mobile UI/UX guidelines and responsive layouts
- Experience integrating mobile apps with REST APIs and third-party libraries
- Proficient in version control using Git
- Bachelor's degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or related field

Nice to have:
- Experience publishing apps to App Store or Google Play
- Familiarity with CI/CD tools for mobile (e.g., Fastlane)

Responsibilities:
- Build and maintain high-performance mobile applications for iOS and Android
- Translate designs and wireframes into high-quality code and layouts
- Collaborate with backend engineers to integrate APIs and ensure app responsiveness
