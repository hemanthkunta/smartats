Job Title: Data Scientist (Machine Learning)

We are seeking a Data Scientist / Machine Learning Engineer with 3+ years of experience to build predictive models and analyze complex datasets.

Requirements:
- 3+ years of experience as a Data Scientist or ML Engineer
- Strong proficiency in Python development
- Solid foundation in Machine Learning concepts and algorithms
- Hands-on experience with frameworks like TensorFlow or PyTorch
- Experience querying databases using SQL
- Proficiency with data manipulation libraries (Pandas, NumPy) and modeling tools (Scikit-Learn)
- Master's or PhD degree in Computer Science, Statistics, Mathematics, or related field

Nice to have:
- Experience with Big Data tools (Spark, Hadoop)
- Familiarity with deploying models in cloud environments

Responsibilities:
- Design, train, and deploy machine learning models to solve business problems
- Clean, analyze, and visualize large datasets to extract actionable insights
- Collaborate with software engineers to integrate model inference APIs
