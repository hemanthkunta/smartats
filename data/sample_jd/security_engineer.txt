Job Title: Cybersecurity Analyst / Security Engineer

We are seeking a Security Engineer with 3+ years of experience to safeguard our networks, systems, and applications from cyber threats.

Requirements:
- 3+ years of experience in cybersecurity, security engineering, or systems security
- Experience with penetration testing, vulnerability scanning, and risk assessment
- Solid understanding of network security protocols and application security (OWASP Top 10)
- Experience configuring Identity and Access Management (IAM) policies
- Hands-on familiarity with Linux security auditing and log analysis
- Proficiency in scripting languages (Python preferred) and version control (Git)
- Bachelor's degree in Cybersecurity, Computer Science, or related field

Nice to have:
- Security certifications like CISSP, CEH, or Security+
- Experience securing cloud infrastructure (AWS)

Responsibilities:
- Identify and patch application vulnerabilities and infrastructure weaknesses
- Monitor networks and systems for security incidents and lead threat response efforts
- Design, build, and enforce security policies across the development lifecycle
